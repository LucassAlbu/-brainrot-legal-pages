Brainrot Local

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

1. Overview

Brainrot Local is a local desktop web app used by one creator. The app stores project files, generated videos, metadata, and publishing queue data locally on the creator's computer.

2. Information processed locally

The app may process local video files, generated scripts, voiceover files, captions, titles, descriptions, hashtags, background assets, music choices, and local job metadata. This data is used to generate videos and manage the local publishing queue.

3. TikTok account data

When the creator connects TikTok, the app uses TikTok OAuth. The app may request basic profile information through the user.info.basic scope to identify the authorized account. The app uses the video.upload scope only to upload selected videos to TikTok drafts/inbox.

4. Tokens and credentials

OAuth tokens and API credentials are stored locally on the creator's computer inside the app's local secrets folder. The app does not ask for or store the creator's TikTok password.

5. Data sharing

The app sends a selected video file and its related title, description, and hashtags to TikTok only when the creator explicitly starts the upload. The app does not sell personal data or operate third-party advertising tracking.

6. Data deletion

The creator can delete local project files, generated videos, publishing queue items, and local OAuth tokens from the computer at any time.
