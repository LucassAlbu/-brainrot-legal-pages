Brainrot Local

Brainrot Local is a private desktop web app used by one creator to generate, organize, and prepare short vertical videos for publishing workflows.

What the app does

The app runs locally on the creator's computer. It helps generate short scripts, combine voiceover, captions, background videos, and music, and prepare a local publishing queue with titles, descriptions, and hashtags.

TikTok integration is used only when the creator chooses a video from the queue and explicitly starts an upload to TikTok drafts/inbox for final review and posting inside TikTok.
